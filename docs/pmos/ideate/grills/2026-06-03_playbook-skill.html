Gaps surfaced (fold into requirements)

  • Topic-similarity over/under-merge — branch-then-topic clustering on title+first-prompt can mis-merge threads; extend the resolver's confirm-the-grouping step to thread boundaries.
  • "Starting prompt" detection — a stated requirement; with thread-merge it is the first genuine human message of the earliest session in the thread, skipping compaction-injected summaries and <command-name> wrappers. Spec it explicitly.
  • Candidate dedup — overlapping threads (a branch + its follow-up HEAD work) could surface as two candidates for one story; needs a merge affordance.
  • Cross-plugin coupling — in pmos-learnkit, /playbook depends on toolkit's command-name/session conventions; light (string/path coupling, not code) but worth an explicit note so it isn't a silent dependency.
  • Screenshot pipeline cost/leak — Playwright in v1 adds a heavy dep and an un-flaggable leak surface gated only by manual review; make the manual-review step a hard, un-skippable gate in the artifact schema.
