## Personas (D2) — derived PER PLUGIN, not collapsed
All personas are **fluent** (terminal, slash commands, Claude Code/Codex) — the through-line is product people who work in the terminal. But the *job* differs per plugin:

- **toolkit (ship):** the founding PM going napkin→MVP · the product lead who needs spec clarity before 5 engineers start · the solo full-stack dev who's great at code, weak in the messy middle.
- **learnkit (learn):** the senior PM ramping on a topic before an exec pitch · the new EM building PM literacy · the founder entering an unfamiliar domain. *Learning, not building — input not output.*
- **managerkit (manage):** the hiring manager / lead interviewer who needs a defensible, cited record after a round.
- **gamekit (break):** the builder mid-deep-work who needs a hard context-switch that won't distract.
- **utilities (maintain):** anyone running the kit who needs to keep machine + skills healthy.
