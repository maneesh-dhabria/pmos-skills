Specification · Tier 3

Inline doc comments

Stakeholders annotate any PMOS-emitted HTML artifact in the browser. Comment threads persist as an inline JSON block inside the HTML itself — no sidecar files; the artifact is the single source of truth. This mockup shows Direction F — Vercel / Geist Minimal applied to a representative spec page.

Context & goals

Pure black & white, hairline borders, lots of negative space, Geist Sans for headings and Geist Mono for the structural marks — with a single Vercel-blue accent used sparingly. Reads as precise, modern, and confident. Closest in spirit to your favourite (C) but sans-first rather than mono-first.

Restraint is the brand: one accent, hairlines, and type that gets out of the way.

Functional requirements

FR-1 — Masthead wordmark

The toolbar carries pmos. in Geist with a blue accent dot. Minimal, geometric, unmistakably modern-tooling. It appears on all 14 HTML surfaces.

FR-2 — Section signature

Each <h2> sits under a hairline rule, prefixed by a zero-padded mono counter (01, 02…) in accent blue. Quiet, systematic, repeats cleanly.
