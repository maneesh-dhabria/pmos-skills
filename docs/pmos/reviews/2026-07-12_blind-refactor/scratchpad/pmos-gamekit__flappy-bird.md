### flappy-bird-F1 [Should-fix] Session best is silently lost on a mid-run restart
- Where: plugins/pmos-gamekit/skills/flappy-bird/game/flappy-bird.html:481
- Quote: "state = E.createState({ width: W, height: H, difficulty: difficulty, seed: null, best: sessionBest });"
- Problem: `sessionBest` is only synced from the run's score inside `onOver()` (line 528, `if (sb > sessionBest) sessionBest = sb;`), but `newGame()` is reachable mid-run via the R/N keys (keydown handler, line 809 `else if (k === 'r' || k === 'R' || k === 'n' || k === 'N') { e.preventDefault(); newGame(); }`). The engine correctly tracks live best (selftest even gates "scoring: best updates with the score"), but the UI layer discards it on restart. One-line fix in `newGame()`: fold `state.best` into `sessionBest` before re-creating state.
