Approach

All five fixes live in one file — plugins/pmos-gamekit/skills/solitaire/game/solitaire.html — which cleanly splits into a pure engine module (window.SolitaireEngine, lines 1–387) and a DOM-guarded UI/render layer (393–820). The plan honors that split (design D7 / Inv-1): everything decidable from state — the productive-move finder and the deadlock scan — goes in the engine with selftest coverage in tests/run.mjs; only outlines, banners, timers, and animation live in the UI. The two cosmetic-only items (header text, draw animation) and the engine core can proceed in parallel; the idle-hint and no-moves banner both consume the engine core, so they follow it.

Risks & notes

  • False deadlock is the main correctness risk — mitigated by the full stock-cycle scan in isDeadlocked (Inv-4), not a single-position check. The TDD case with a winnable deal guards against it.
  • Animation race on rapid draws — require FLIP measure/transition to be interruptible and idempotent against render(); final DOM must equal a plain re-render.
  • Card-back legibility at the mobile breakpoint — verify contrast/coverage; keep designs CSS/SVG-inline (Inv-3, no assets).
  • Idle delay (8s) and back-cover count (~6) are tunable per the design Open-Questions; implementer settles them by feel during the self-check.
