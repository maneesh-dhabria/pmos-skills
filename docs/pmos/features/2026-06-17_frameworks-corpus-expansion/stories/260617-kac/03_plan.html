Approach

Remove the Notion sync feature and ship the research/authoring process as reference docs, leaving direct authoring as the single corpus-maintenance path. The work is ordered around one load-bearing constraint: validate-corpus.mjs imports DECISION_TYPES / LIFECYCLE_STAGES / validateAnchors from derive-fields.mjs (a sync-pipeline file). T1 preserves those exports and re-passes --selftest before any deletion happens. After that the removals (scripts, tests, SKILL.md surface, ingestion.md) and the new corpus-expansion.md are independent edits, closed out by a grep-clean + full regression.
